## Strategic Fit & Market Opportunity

MyHealthcare operates in the Indian digital health space, focusing on an integrated ecosystem that combines SaaS (EMR, CDSS) with a marketplace (diagnostics, pharmacy, chronic care). The market opportunity is substantial, given India's fragmented healthcare infrastructure and the growing demand for digital health solutions. The company's focus on chronic care management (diabetes, cardiology) addresses a high-volume, high-retention segment.

However, the market is highly competitive, with established players like Practo, Innovaccer, and Athenahealth, as well as numerous regional and niche players. MyHealthcare's differentiation lies in its AI-powered, data-driven approach and its integrated ecosystem model. Yet, the company's ability to capture and retain market share is uncertain, given the high churn rates and integration complexities mentioned in the documents.
