## Executive Summary

**Verdict:** Hold (with strong Buy potential pending data room access and model validation)

**Top 3 Reasons**
1. **High Capital Efficiency & Proven Unit Economics:** Just Dogs demonstrates a 3.5x revenue-to-capital ratio, significantly outperforming peers (0.6x). Franchisee payback periods are short (12-15 months), and the business model is inherently capital-light for the company, though shifting to COCO requires upfront capex.
2. **Structural Margin Expansion via Private Labels:** The company is strategically pivoting from low-margin third-party brand distribution to high-margin private labels (65%+ gross margins). Private label revenue is growing at a 68% 3-year CAGR and is projected to scale from ~10% to a much larger share of the portfolio.
3. **Market Tailwinds & Omnichannel Leadership:** Operating in a fragmented, largely unorganized market, Just Dogs is the first profitable omnichannel player in India's pet care sector. The market is projected to grow from $14bn to $32bn by 2026, driven by rising pet humanization and increased calorie consumption per pet.

## Strategic Fit & Market Opportunity

*   **Market Size & Growth:** The Indian pet care market is estimated at $14bn (2021) and projected to reach $32bn by 2026. Key drivers include a growing pet population (32m to 56m) and a shift from 7% to 10% calorie conversion from human food to pet food.
*   **Market Penetration:** Just Dogs currently holds only 6% market penetration, indicating significant headroom for growth.
*   **Competitive Landscape:** The market is fragmented with competition from:
    *   **FMCG Incumbents:** Large pet food manufacturers with distribution networks.
    *   **Specialty Chains:** Competitors like 'Heads Up for Tails' (raised INR 150 crore, higher valuation, but unprofitable).
    *   **E-commerce Giants:** Amazon dominates online sales (80% share), though Just Dogs leverages its omnichannel presence to differentiate.
    *   **Vet Clinics & Unorganized Retail:** Traditional channels still hold significant share.
*   **Strategic Positioning:** Just Dogs differentiates through its hybrid COCO/FOFO model, strong franchisee partnerships, and control over sourcing and customer data. The shift toward COCO stores aims to capture higher margins and improve brand consistency.

## Operational Due Diligence

*   **Business Model:** Hybrid of Company-Owned Company-Operated (COCO) and Franchise-Owned Franchise-Operated (FOFO) stores. The company is strategically shifting toward COCO for better margin control and operational consistency.
*   **Store Network:** Operates 44+ stores across 16+ cities, with 57-58% located in Tier 2+ cities. Target is to scale to ~180 stores.
*   **Unit Economics:**
    *   **Payback Period:** 12-15 months for franchisees.
    *   **Breakeven:** Within 6 months of launch.
    *   **Revenue per Sq Ft:** INR 2,100 (COCO) vs. INR 1,050 (FOFO).
    *   **Store EBITDA:** Average store EBITDA is 20%+, though there is significant variance (e.g., Pune at 17.62% vs. Bangalore HSR at -25.04%).
*   **Product Strategy:** Sells 6,000+ SKUs across 8 categories. Private labels include Aroma Groom, Snackers, Ezra, and Harleys. Own-brand revenue share was ~10% in FY21, growing at a 68% 3-year CAGR.
*   **Services:** Grooming services with 80+ groomers and a loyalty program with 1.6L+ registered members. Spa operations contribute ~5% of revenue but are vulnerable to lockdowns/health crises.
*   **Customer Metrics:**
    *   **Repeat Buyer Rate:** 70-80% offline.
    *   **Customer Base:** 2.5L+ registered customers.
    *   **AOV:** INR 2,200+ with a basket size of 6+ items.
*   **Omnichannel Presence:** Sales split is approximately 53% offline and 24% online. Online revenue is projected to reach INR 10 crore in FY22E.

## Financial Deep Dive

*   **Projected Performance:**

*   **Fundraising:** Seeking INR 1,300 Mn (USD 15 Mn) for growth capital, expansion, e-commerce enhancement, and own brands.
