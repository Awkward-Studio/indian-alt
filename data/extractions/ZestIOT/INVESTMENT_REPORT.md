## Operational Due Diligence

- **Product Model:** The product is customized per client, which may impact scalability compared to pure SaaS models. This customization is a key differentiator but also a potential bottleneck for rapid scaling.
- **Customer Concentration:** While customer concentration risk is noted as low, there is a reliance on specific sectors, particularly aviation. This sector-specific focus could be a vulnerability if the aviation industry faces downturns.
- **Contract Terms:** Contracts typically range from 3 to 5 years, providing a degree of revenue visibility.
- **Revenue Mix:** 60-70% of revenue comes from licensing, with the remainder likely from services. This mix suggests a recurring revenue component but also dependency on service delivery.

## Financial Deep Dive

- **Profitability:** The company is profitable with gross margins of 75%+.
- **Cash Position:** Described as "flushed with cash," indicating strong liquidity and low immediate capital needs.
- **Funding History:** IvyCap Ventures invested INR 50 crore in March 2023 for a total round size of INR 51 crore. Funds were allocated to sales expansion in Europe/US and engineering improvements.
- **Inferred Conclusions:** The high margins and cash position suggest a mature, capital-efficient business. However, the customized nature of the product may limit the speed of revenue growth compared to pure software plays.

## Risk Matrix (Top 5 Risks)

1. **Promoter Exit Reluctance:** The promoter (Amit) may not be open to a full sale, complicating exit strategies. *Mitigation:* Structure the deal to allow for partial exits or secondary sales to other investors.
2. **Customized Product Scalability:** The product is customized per client, which may impact scalability. *Mitigation:* Assess the degree of customization vs. standardization and the engineering resources required for each client.
3. **Sector Concentration:** Reliance on aviation, FMCG, Steel, and Coal sectors. *Mitigation:* Diversify customer base within these sectors and monitor sector-specific risks.
4. **Revenue Mix Dependency:** 60-70% revenue from licensing, with the rest from services. *Mitigation:* Ensure service delivery is efficient and does not become a cost center.
5. **Capital Requirement & Secondary Availability:** The need for capital and availability of secondary buyers. *Mitigation:* Evaluate the company's cash burn rate and potential secondary market interest.
